About Datt

Datt is a community where people view great content and pay the creators, and people post great content and get paid for their creations.

The business model of Datt is to take a portion of payments between users rather than displaying advertisements, which better aligns the incentives of the company with the interests of the users.

Our goal is to empower users to find the best content, to properly own their content, and to get paid for the value they produce.

Datt is under development. Follow our blog for progress updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I want to pay creators?

Because you believe in what the creators are saying and want to support their efforts, and you want their content to be more visible than other content.

How do I get paid?

Users pay each other bitcoin for creating content. The company and users take a portion of payments for work such as hosting or moderating content.

Why is Datt better than reddit, Twitter, or Facebook?

Datt has better content, you get paid for the work that you do, and you don't have to view targeted advertisements or give up your privacy.

Why is Datt Decentralized?

Users should own their content and shouldn't have to view advertisements or sell their privacy to use an app. By building on a decentralized protocol, our technology is aligned with our values.

Do I need to install anything?

No, Datt runs in a web browser and can be used as easily as any web app. This is possible thanks to modern web technologies such as Web RTC, IndexedDB, and web workers.
